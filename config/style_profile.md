# Russian Style Profile

Derived from eight published Shorts on 2026-07-16. Use the reference texts in `references/my_texts/` as the final authority.

## Voice

- Russian, direct second-person address, natural and conversational.
- Curious and confident, without academic wording or long caveats.
- The narration uses short declarative sentences and simple explanations.
- A small reaction or contrast can reinforce a surprising detail: "это же просто гениально", "для нас это непривычно".
- Do not make broad claims about every person in a country. Attribute relationship or culture observations carefully.

## Structure

1. Hook in the first sentence: "Три вещи...", "Топ-четыре...", or a direct question.
2. Three facts by default; use four only when every point is strong and visual.
3. Each fact has a concrete example and one short explanation of why it matters.
4. Finish with one simple question for comments. A like/follow prompt is optional and must not interrupt a fact.

## Length And Pace

- Published references range from 95 to 163 Russian words.
- Default target: 100-130 words, about 40-55 seconds in this delivery.
- Four-point scripts may reach 145-160 words, about 55-65 seconds.
- Keep each fact compact enough to show visually.

## Avoid

- Long introductions and Wikipedia-like explanations.
- Repeating any detail in `data/blocked_details.json`.
- Copying the source wording or fact order too closely.
- Unverifiable universal claims.
